About The Game

Wall Defense is the first game to ever be developed by Procrastination software.  We are a development team based in Towson, MD made up of undergraduate students looking to break into the gaming industry with a bang. Wall defense was developed in under 6 months from conception to initial release. As the first game in what is hopefully a long list of titles coming from this company. It was a real learning experience for all of us as for may of us it was our first time in a software development team. This game takes a lot of its inspiration from other popular indie games including Geometry Wars and Super Hexagon. It has changed drastically from our original sketch.
